Engineering Week @ UMBC

Logo Design
Project Summary
My submission for a T-shirt Design contest held at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. The contest was part of celebrating Engineer's Week at the university. I designed the front and back of the t-shirt while keeping space for additional university sponsor logos on the back of the tee.

The overall idea was to communicate the coalescence of all branches of engineering to drive innovation.
